SmartSpend – Phase 1 Summary
=============================

Dataset Overview
----------------
The dataset was loaded from credit_card_transactions.csv and consists of 1296641 records and 29 features. All columns were reviewed for completeness and data quality.

Data Cleaning
-------------
During the cleaning process, 0 duplicate records were removed and 0 invalid amount records were filtered out. 34 unrealistic amount records were removed (amounts below $0.01 or above $10,000). Missing values were imputed using the mean for numeric columns and the mode for categorical columns. The final cleaned dataset contains 1296641 valid records.

Feature Engineering
-------------------
The following features were added: Month, Year, DayOfWeek, and IsWeekend. The data spans from 2019-01 to 2020-06. Approximately 34.8% of transactions occurred on weekends and 65.2% occurred on weekdays.

Amount Statistics
-----------------
After cleaning, the transaction amount column has the following distribution: Minimum: $1.00, Maximum: $9999.39, Mean: $69.96, Standard Deviation: $138.42.
